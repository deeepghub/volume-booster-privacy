Privacy Policy for Volume Booster

Volume Booster is a browser extension designed to enhance audio playback volume and enable fullscreen streaming on platforms like YouTube and Twitch.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All functionality runs locally within your browser. No personal information, browsing history, or communications are accessed.

Independence

Volume Booster is completely independent. It does not connect to any external servers or databases. All operations are performed on your device only.

Permissions

The extension requests limited permissions (activeTab, storage, scripting, tabs) solely to provide its core functionality. These permissions are used locally and never involve external data transfer.
